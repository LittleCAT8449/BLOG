## Why This Blog Exists

After years of consuming content, I decided it was time to start creating. This blog is my space to share thoughts, document learnings, and connect with like-minded people.

Writing is one of the best ways to clarify your thinking. When you write something down, you're forced to organize your thoughts, fill in the gaps, and make your ideas concrete.

## What to Expect

I'll be writing about a variety of topics:

- **Technology** — Web development, tools, and software engineering practices
- **Design** — UI/UX thoughts, visual design principles, and creative processes
- **Life** — Personal experiences, productivity, and reflections

## The Tech Stack

This blog is built with modern web technologies:

- **Next.js 15** with the App Router
- **TypeScript** for type safety
- **Tailwind CSS** for styling
- **Markdown** for content authoring

The entire site is statically generated, making it blazing fast.

I hope you find something useful here. Feel free to reach out through the comments or social links — I'd love to hear from you!
